Welcome to the Restaurant Recommender!

To view recommendations, you can either use custom coordinates, your own location, or a custom search. If you choose to use your own location, please first click the "Enable Location" button and click accept to allow us to use HTML Geolocation to find where to recommend restaurants near you. Then click the "Access Recommendations Using Your Coordinates" button to view what we recommend. If you want to use custom coordinates, enter your latitude and longitude into the respective spots on the form and then hit "Submit." For a custom search, type the location you desire and hit "Submit." Note that these options only search by location. To access the about page, click the "About" button above.
